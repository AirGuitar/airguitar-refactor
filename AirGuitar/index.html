AirGuitar

Pushing Guitar Pedals to the Cloud.

AirGuitar is a digital guitar pedal service that allows guitar enthusiasts to customize, produce, and share their music. We provide a growing library of guitar pedals ready for selection and use. With AirGuitar, users can select and combine different pedals to produce a wide range of tone and music. We also grant access to additional facilities for recording and storing music tracks. With all these features, AirGuitar helps users find their own unique sound for the world to hear.

Pick your Pedals

Browse and pick from a growing library of guitar pedals. Whether you're pursuing a jazzy tone or an aggresive metal-like crunch, we have a wide range of pedals to fulfil your needs.

Customize your Sound

Customize your sound by combining different types of pedals. If you want further customization, feel free to toggle individual pedal settings to find the right blend of tone.

Record your Tracks

Record and store noteworthy music tracks through our virtual studio. It doesn't matter if it's a quick guitar riff or a full-blown instrutmental solo, we've got you covered.
